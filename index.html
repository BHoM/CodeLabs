Programme Growth Timeline and Outcomes

Building sustainable computational communities across all regions through structured phases

Day 0

Foundation Building

Community building with regular attendance and enthusiastic early adopters supported by the Computational Engineering team. Regional identification - mapping discipline coding leads and emerging computational champions.

Day 200

Transition Phase

Hybrid leadership - regional leads begin co-facilitating sessions with Computational Engineering team support. Discipline specific focus and peer learning with experienced attendees mentoring newer participants.

Day 500

Maturity & Organic Growth

Regional Ownership - local leaders host their own sessions. Computational Engineering team as consultancy model providing strategic guidance. Self-sustaining culture with computational innovation embedded in regional practices.
